Methodology & Contact

Architecture-first, compliance-aware engagements that move from discovery to operate with clear checkpoints.

How we work

A five-step approach that balances structured governance with practical cloud delivery.

Step 1

Discover

  • Clarify business drivers and constraints.
  • Map key stakeholders and decision forums.
  • Identify regulatory requirements in scope.

Step 2

Assess

  • Review current architecture, controls and processes.
  • Highlight risks, gaps and quick wins.
  • Agree target outcomes and priorities.

Step 3

Architect

  • Design target cloud and security architectures.
  • Align patterns to frameworks such as SOC 2 and ISO 27001.
  • Define migration or implementation paths.

Step 4

Implement

  • Support delivery teams with patterns and guardrails.
  • Embed security and compliance checks into pipelines.
  • Validate changes against agreed controls.

Step 5

Operate

  • Define runbooks, monitoring and escalation paths.
  • Review incident and change processes for alignment.
  • Plan continuous improvement cycles.
